Hi!
I'm Xuan

I'm a sophomore at the University of Washington Bothell who has a great passion for art and design. Welcome to my portfolio for the Interactive Media Design Application! Being at UW Bothell has been an opportunity that I have always appreciated. I believe UWB is the fundamental base for me to follow my dream of becoming a professional in the art and design field.

I started to associate with art when I was 8 years old. I expressed my interest in fine art when taking a kid's art class. I was a quiet kid but had a creative mind. Starting with some basic drawing and painting with a pencil and crayons, I created many artworks using my imagination. I didn't know when I fell in love with art. I knew that I was attached to it, and I wanted it to be a part of my life. I decided to follow the path as an art lover and wanted to pursue an art career. Besides traditional drawing and painting, I started learning digital art, photography, and coding (recently). Having a strong will and mind, I believe I am the person who can do and will do anything I want.
