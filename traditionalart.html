Art work

My mom sent me to a kid's art class since I was 8 years old. I didn't quite remember what I did in the class black then. The only thing I could remember was the encouragement and the compliment from my mom and I kept doing art since then. When I was back in Vietname, my teachers and people around me kept saying things like following art (which is my dream) or become an artist or designer could not make me rich.
Schools forced me to study the subjects like Math, Chemistry, or Physics which I didn't like and I wasn't good at either. However, I kept begging my mom to allow me to take extra art classes (schools in Vietnam don't teach art as an optional subject). I am a lucky person who has a mom that supports my dream. Art somehow becomes a part of my life. Painting, drawing, and creating are not simply a hobby for me anymore, they are the dream that I want to follow. Hopefully one day I can be successful with the path that I choose. I usually do traditional painting and drawing. But since I came to America and had a chance to approach new techonologies,
I started self-teaching me digital art. Even though I'm still not mastering the skill, I'm trying my best to practice everyday
to learn digital art and prove to other people that I have the ability to do everything I want.
