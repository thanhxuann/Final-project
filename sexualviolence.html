This series is the result of my AP Art class in high school. I intended to explore gender and sexuality topics. However, there was much news about sexual violence and harassment in Vietnam, the country where I grew up. I thought it would be an excellent opportunity to express the issue and raise awareness using my art. I started by reading many news articles discussing the issue and tried to use symbols and objects to address the content of my art rather than sketching realistic stories. I hope more people will notice the problems and have stricter laws and regulations to prevent sexual violence and harassment.
