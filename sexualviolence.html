This series explores the aesthetics of photography. The term “aesthetic” is defined as a branchgo of philosophy that deals with the nature of beauty and taste, as well as the philosophy of art (its own area of philosophy that comes out of aesthetics). It examines aesthetic values, often expressed through judgments of taste. I used the film simulation setting of my Fujifilm XT-100 camera instead of using an actual film camera. I love playing with different setups when taking pictures as well as playing with the colors of the objects in the photo. With a hint of editing using Lightroom (temperature and exposure), I wanted to bring a vintage mixed with a modern vibe for the series.
